Capstone Project Overview

This capstone project involved designing a mobile app for Fischer Investments to support students transitioning from college into the professional workforce. The app targets graduating seniors and recent graduates, addressing common challenges such as anxiety, uncertainty, and lack of professional confidence. Through features like mentorship, job listings, event discovery, and calendar integration, the app creates a supportive ecosystem that helps users navigate the early stages of their careers with clarity and confidence.

Problem

This capstone project involved designing a mobile app for Fischer Investments to support students transitioning from college into the professional workforce. The app targets graduating seniors and recent graduates, addressing common challenges such as anxiety, uncertainty, and lack of professional confidence. Through features like mentorship, job listings, event discovery, and calendar integration, the app creates a supportive ecosystem that helps users navigate the early stages of their careers with clarity and confidence.

Solution

To address this challenge, we designed a mobile app specifically for Fischer Investments that supports students and recent graduates during their transition into the workforce. We called it 'Compass Connect'. The app focuses on reducing anxiety by providing guidance, structure, and connection. By combining mentorship, career resources, and scheduling tools into one intuitive platform, the app helps users feel supported and informed as they take their first steps into their professional careers. Our solution emphasizes accessibility, clarity, and reassurance, creating a smoother transition from school life to the workplace.

Research

Our research included exploring existing studies and articles about post-graduation anxiety and early-career stress. Research shows that many recent graduates experience imposter syndrome, fear of failure, and uncertainty about professional norms when entering the workforce. Studies also indicate that access to mentorship and clear career guidance significantly reduces anxiety and improves confidence among early-career professionals. Additionally, research highlighted that structured support systems, such as career planning tools and networking opportunities, help graduates feel more prepared and engaged. These findings reinforced the importance of building an app that prioritizes mentorship, organization, and transparent communication.

Interviews

Applicants were being ignored and rejected due to lack of experience. These factors lead to more anxiety and disconnect within the job process. We conducted interviews with soon to be graduates or current graduates who are in the job market. We learned that applicants sought connections and worried about being the proper fit for a job. Responses from interviewee's includes, "Don’t just apply, also make connections and network too", "Being rejected makes it hard to keep going”, “Will this role support my journey and long-term goals?”, and Applying is not enough, networking is a must."

Features

The Fischer Investments app includes several key features designed to support recent graduates. Users can connect with mentors through in-app messaging or schedule video calls for guidance and career advice. A job listings section allows users to browse relevant opportunities within Fischer Investments. The integrated calendar feature helps users track upcoming interviews, meetings, and deadlines, reducing stress through organization. Additionally, the events tab enables users to discover upcoming Fischer events and career fairs, with the ability to add events directly to their calendar. Together, these features create a comprehensive support system that empowers users to confidently transition into the professional workplace.
