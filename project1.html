MyFitt App

For this project, I designed an app dedicated to health and wellness. MyFitt is a fitness and nutrition tracking app designed to help users reach their health goals. The app allows users to log meals, track macronutrients, and discover personalized workout routines. With an intuitive interface and motivational features, MyFitt makes maintaining a healthy lifestyle simple and enjoyable.
